Floor Money

Floor money is money you can earn for your floor or hall to fund fun events. You can earn floor money by participating in events such as hallympics and Greatest Floor. You can also earn floor money by having at least 2 people from your floor attend the weekly RHA meetings held at 6:15 on Wednesdays in the Faculty Dining Room.
