PROPOSALS

Do you have a fun idea for an event that you want your floor to do? Send a proposal to RHA and it will be reviewed to potentially become a sponsored event! When submitting a proposal, be sure to include the following in your e-mail:

  • Include the name of your hall / organization and the dollar amount of the proposal in the subject line.
  • Attach a powerpoint with the following information to the email:
    • Who will be able to participate in the event
    • What the event is
    • When the event will be held
    • Where the event will be held
    • The dollar amount of the proposal
  • Any additional information that you want RHA exec to know about your proposal

Please remember that all proposals must be submitted Monday by 6:15PM.
