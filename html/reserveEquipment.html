Reservation

Interested in renting some equipment from RHA? Select from the drop down below and view its availability!

RHA has recently opened up their equipment for reservation by students and faculty. Here you are able to select and view the available times that a piece of equipment is available. Some things to know before renting:


  • You may only rent one piece of equipment at a time.
  • Each item may only be reserved for at most 3 days.
  • You can reserve an item at most two weeks in advance.
  • Late penalties may be enforced, varying on the demand of the item.
  • All equipment must be returned by 5:00PM on its return date.
